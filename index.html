Mediterranean Cod

This cod recipe is inspired by the flavors and ingredients of the Mediterranean region.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of cod
  • Baby tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 fresh lemons
  • 4 sprigs of thyme

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. Pour the olive oil on a baking dish.
  3. Place the cod in a baking dish.
  4. Put baby tomatoes on top.
  5. Slice the lemons and put them on top of the baby tomatoes.
  6. Place one sprig of thyme on top of each piece of cod.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes.
